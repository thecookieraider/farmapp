The Question

When we began brainstorming this application, farmers were at the front of our minds. What was something within our reach that we could create to aid farmers in a significant way? It had to be a tool that would simplify an almost daily activity; What is an activity vital to keeping a farm up and running that is done daily?

Our Goal

Farms can have hundreds of items to keep track of; any where from cows and chickens to seeds and haystacks. Being able to keep track of it all must be a pain without decent software! That’s were we come in. We designed an application meant to alleviate as much of the burden of tracking everything on a farm. We did so by keeping in mind what a farmer could possibly need to keep track of. Everything from livestock vaccines and medical records to their lineage and Buttercup’s birthday.
